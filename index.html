Workouts & Nutrition Account Deletion Instructions

This page explains how users can request deletion of their Workouts & Nutrition account and associated data.

Step-by-Step

  1. Contact the developer at: urvandimon@gmail.com
  2. Provide your account details (username, email, or phone number) to verify your account.
  3. Request deletion of your account and associated personal data.
  4. All personal data stored in Firebase (name, weight, height, activity, uploaded media) will be deleted.
  5. Deletion will be completed within 30 days from your request.

Workouts & Nutrition is committed to user privacy and compliance with data protection laws.
